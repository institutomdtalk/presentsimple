Phase 04

He, She, It — DOES

IV
Third person singular — the verb changes, the auxiliary changes
AffirmativeShe / Lilian works at the hospital.
QuestionDOES she work at the hospital?
NegativeShe doesn't work at the hospital.
—
Clinical examples — Laura & Sofia
PluralDO Laura and Sofia like playing together? → they
SingularDOES Laura fall asleep easily?
AnswerYes, she does.
SingularDOES Sofia sleep all night?
AnswerYes, she does.
PluralDO they sleep together?
AnswerYes, they do.
Golden Rule — Regra de Ouro

Whenever we have an auxiliary — DO, DOES, DID, WILL, WOULD + modal verbs — the main verb will always be in its neutral (base) form.

Regra Importante

Com DOES, o verbo não recebe -s:
She works → DOES she work?

Whose — Dupla Função

Pergunta: de quem
"Whose coat is this?"

Em frases: cujo/cuja
"Lilian, whose coat is here, went home."

How vs. How Much/Many

how much → incontáveis
how many → contáveis
how often → frequência
