Phase 04

He, She, It — DOES

AffirmativeShe / Lilian works at the hospital.
QuestionDOES she work at the hospital?
NegativeShe doesn't work at the hospital.
PluralDO Laura and Sofia like playing together? → they
SingularDOES Laura fall asleep easily?
AnswerYes, she does.
SingularDOES Sofia sleep all night?
AnswerYes, she does.
PluralDO they sleep together?
AnswerYes, they do.
Regra de Ouro

Whenever we have an auxiliary — DO, DOES, DID, WILL, WOULD + modal verbs — the main verb will always be in its neutral (base) form.
